# IncomeBase

**IncomeBase** is an enterprise AI underwriting engine that transforms unstructured financial documents from self-employed borrowers into verified, lender-ready income calculations.

---

## The Problem

Traditional income verification for self-employed borrowers is slow, manual, and error-prone. Lenders must chase down documents, manually review PDFs and images, and try to reconstruct income from a patchwork of statements, W2s, and other uploads. This process is:

- **Time-consuming:** Underwriters spend hours or days per borrower.
- **Inconsistent:** Manual review leads to errors and missed risk factors.
- **Friction-filled:** Borrowers are confused by what to upload and when.
- **Risky:** Fraud, missing pages, and outdated documents are hard to catch.

---

## The Solution

IncomeBase automates and streamlines the entire pipeline:

1. **Lender creates a secure upload link** for the borrower.
2. **Borrower verifies their identity** (ZIP or SSN) before uploading.
3. **Borrower uploads documents** (bank statements, W2s, etc.) via a simple drag-and-drop interface.
4. **AI instantly classifies and checks documents** for completeness, recency, and fraud risk—giving real-time feedback if something is missing or unclear.
5. **Underwriter is notified** the moment uploads are complete.
6. **One-click analysis** reconstructs income, aggregates stats, and produces a standardized, auditable report.
7. **Lender-ready output**: All results are available in a modern dashboard, ready for underwriting or export.

---

## Key Features

- **Automated Document Parsing:** Handles PDFs, images, and more.
- **AI-Powered Classification & Feedback:** Instantly detects missing or problematic files.
- **Identity Verification:** Ensures only the right borrower can upload.
- **Real-Time Underwriter Dashboard:** See borrower status, document completeness, and risk at a glance.
- **One-Click Income Analysis:** Aggregates, analyzes, and explains income for even the most complex self-employed cases.
- **Audit Trail:** Every step and decision is logged for compliance.

## Who Is It For?

- **Fintechs** building modern mortgage or loan origination flows.
- **Borrowers** who want a fast, guided, and transparent document submission experience.

---

## How It Works (Workflow)

1. **Create Link:** Lender generates a secure upload link for a borrower.
2. **Verify Identity:** Borrower verifies ZIP or SSN before uploading.
3. **Upload Docs:** Borrower uploads required documents; AI checks for completeness.
4. **Instant Feedback:** If a page is missing or unreadable, the borrower is notified immediately.
5. **Analysis:** Underwriter clicks “Analyze” to get a full income breakdown and risk summary.
6. **Decision:** Lender reviews, exports, or requests more info—all from one dashboard.
